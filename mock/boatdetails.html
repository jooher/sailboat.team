Yacht description

5-cabin sailing yacht Dufour 460 - Hummingbird was built in 2019 and it is docked in DYC Olbia Marina, Italy. Hummingbird can accommodate up to 12 people in 4 double cabins, 1 double cabin with a bunk bed and a saloon with 2 extra beds. Pillows and blankets are provided during the sailing yacht rental.

Sailing yacht Hummingbird offers 3 toilets and a shower.

Dufour features Classical mainsail, BBQ, Bimini and Outside GPS plotter. It also boasts Audio system, Outside speakers and Inside speakers. Fully-equipped kitchen includes Oven, Cooker, Sink and Kitchen utensils.

This sailing yacht is operated in Italy by charter Dream Yacht Charter - Italy.

Information about the marina

DYC Olbia Marina is situated nearby Olbia, Italy. It is located 4 kilometres from the nearest airport and 1 kilometre from Olbia city centre. Taxis from the airport cost approximately 15 €.

DYC Olbia Marina boasts a wide range of facilities for sailors, including a car park.

It also offers a restaurant and a supermarket.
